OUR STORY

BEGINNINGS

First meeting while both working for Metropolitan Market, Arra and Joe quickly bonded over their shared love of awful puns. After months of group board game nights (and one legendary workplace christmas party), the sparks were flying.

Days turned to weeks turned to months, and Joe and Arra realized this was something special. While on vacation in Oregon, Arra confessed to Joe that she loved him. Joe said the same. Love was in the air.

THE PROPOSAL

One day, shortly after returning from his sister Rene√©'s wedding, Joe decided he wanted to marry Arra. He took her hand, told her how much she loves him, how he could spend every waking moment with her and it stll wouldn't be enough(I know, barf barf), and how happy he would be to spend the rest of his life with her. She agreed.

THE EVENT

ODD FELLOWS HALL

Located in the heart of Eastsound (the largest town on Orcas Island) with stunning views overlooking Puget Sound, Odd Fellows Hall has been hosting weddings and community gatherings for over 100 years. The ceremony will be held on the back patio under the madrona trees overlooking the water, the reception inside the hall with patio access so guests can continue to enjoy the absolutely gorgeous views.
