THE EVENT

ODD FELLOWS HALL

Located in the heart of Eastsound (the largest town on Orcas Island) with stunning views overlooking Puget Sound, Odd Fellows Hall has been hosting weddings and community gatherings for over 100 years. The ceremony will be held on the back patio under the madrona trees overlooking the water, the reception inside the hall with patio access so guests can continue to enjoy the absolutely gorgeous views.

DIRECTIONS

BY CAR

you will need to take a ferry to Orcas island. If you plan on taking a vehicle on the ferry, it is strongly advised that you make a reservation. The reservations for the ferries are released in tiers. Tier 1: 30% of spaces made available 2 months prior to the season start*, Tier 2: Another 30% of spaces is made available 2 weeks prior to the sail date; Tier 3: The final 30% of spaces will be available 2 days prior to sailing. *Tall reservation space for large, non-standard vehicles is all released with tier 1. For the Summer 2020 Season, reservations will become available on April 21, 2020. Visit the WSDOT Ferry website to make your reseravtion.

BY PLANE

Chartered Flights: The chartered flight will take you directly from Boeing Field to East Sound in Orcas Island. More info here
